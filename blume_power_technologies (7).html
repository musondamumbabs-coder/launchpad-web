How It Works

Simple. Fast. Reliable.

Z-Alert works silently in the background — springing to life the moment a threat is detected.

01
🔌
Install the Sensor
Attach the Z-Alert sensor to your cable or power line. Simple installation, no specialist tools needed.
›
02
📡
GSM Connects
Insert a local SIM card. The device connects to the GSM network and begins monitoring 24/7.
›
03
⚡
Disturbance Detected
The moment cable is cut, moved, or tampered with, the sensor triggers instantly — day or night.
›
04
📱
SMS Alert Fires
You receive an instant SMS on your phone. Act fast — contact security, police, or staff immediately.
